Guidelines on picking similar articles

Same story (YES)

You should choose this when these articles are obviously treating the same story, possibly with a different slant.

You should be able to answer yes to both of these questions:

  • Could these two articles have the same title?
  • Is the main point of each article covered also in the other article?

Strongly related stories (ALMOST)

You should choose this when the two articles are strictly speaking not treating the same story, but are otherwise strongly related.

You should be able to answer yes to both of these questions, while answering no to the previous questions:

  • Could the content of one of two articles be added seamlessly to the other article?
  • Can you think of at least two common tags for these two articles?
