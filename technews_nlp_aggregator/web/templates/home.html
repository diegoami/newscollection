Welcome to TechControversy.com !

This site aggregates articles from technology news portals and tries to find out those that are reporting on the same story, possibly having a different slant.

You can also see similar stories side to side to compare what different news outlets are saying about the same story. And help us validate results in the process.

You can also enter directly the url of an article in the database and find out the most related stories in the database. Or just go to a random article.

You can also paste here the text of a technical article from another source and find the related articles in the database.

NLP for single articles can be retrieved here.

More information can be found in the Github repository.
