Welcome to Tech News Controversies!

This page aggregates articles from technology news portals and tries to find out those that are reporting on the same story.

You can also see similar storie side to side to compare what different news outlet are saying about the same story. And help us validate results in the process.

You can also enter directly the url of an article in the database and find out the most related stories in the database. Or just go to a random article.

You can also paste the text of a technical article from another source and find the related articles in the database.

More information can be found in the Github repository.
